Qlikdeals

  • Qlikdeals.com is a price comparison platform which allows customers to find out the best products available across a wide spectrum of categories. Theses products include Personal Care, Electronics, Mobiles, and Fashion & Lifestyle.
  • The Qlikdeals.com portal is created with the primary goal of giving customer an opportunity of wide selection as an optimum online discovery and shopping research destination.
  • Qlikdeals.com portal will serve as a search engine that is vertical for use of shoppers so that they can compare and filter products they seek based on the criteria of features and price.
  • It also provides users a feature to set a price alert notification on a product for desired price.
